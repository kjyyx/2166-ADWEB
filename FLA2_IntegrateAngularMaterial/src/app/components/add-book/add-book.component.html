Know HAU

Holy Angel University is a private Catholic university located in Angeles City, Pampanga, Philippines. It was founded in 1933 by layperson Don Juan Nepomuceno and co-founded by Fr. Pedro Paulo Santos. The university offers a wide range of academic programs across various disciplines, including business, education, engineering, arts and sciences, hospitality management, and more. Holy Angel University is known for its strong emphasis on holistic education, character formation, and community engagement. It has a vibrant campus life with cultural events, sports activities, and student organizations contributing to a well-rounded educational experience.
